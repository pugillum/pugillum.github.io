About me

April 10, 2021 · 1 min

So who is this baldy beardy guy?

I have worked in many roles ending with “Engineer” but currently enjoy the role of Machine Learning Engineer.

Software is much more about people than technology which is why I’m quite a fan of what’s expressed in the Agile Manifesto (and not so much of “Agile” being shoe-horned into organizations).

Some more about me:

  • was born and raised in South Africa
  • husband and father of 2 lovely daughters
  • I love solving puzzles (fortunately a lot of these are in my job)
  • I studied mechatronics engineering but transitioned into software (like so many engineers)
  • I live on the outskirts of Amsterdam (and love many things about the Netherlands)
  • I love beer and books, preferably combined with a beach chair and some sun
  • sailing is awesome

This blog is an attempt to get me to practice writing more and finding a bit more courage in sharing my learnings/wisdom/failures with others.
